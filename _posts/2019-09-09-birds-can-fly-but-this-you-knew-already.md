# 8D-Audio

### What is 8D audio?

![](RackMultipart20201124-4-sz50xl_html_6be22c2ec93861ea.jpg)

8D music is a type of audio that allows you to listen to songs or music from 8 different directions. These types of sounds are also known as Ambisonic Audio and are mainly used for VR programs to create a more realistic experience.
